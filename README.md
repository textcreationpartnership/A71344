    _ Weſtminſter.

    _ Advertiſements of Books newly Printed and Publiſhed.

    _ From Argileſhire in ScotlandAug. 13. 1660.

    _ Whitehall.

    _ Weſtminſter.

    _ Books lately Printed and Publiſhed.

    _ ADVERTISEMENT.

    _ Warſovia in Poland,Aug. 1.

    _ From Brementhe 5th. of Auguſt.
